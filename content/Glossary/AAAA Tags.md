# Philosophy & Faith

*Philosophy & Faith*

 Philosophy: The Search for Truth & Meaning
Philosophy is the study of big questions like:
 What is real?
 What is the meaning of life?
 What is good and evil?
 What is free will?

 Branches of Philosophy:
 Ethics - The study of right and wrong.
 Logic - The rules of clear thinking.
 Metaphysics - Understanding reality beyond science.
 Epistemology - The study of knowledge and truth.

 Example: If a tree falls in the forest and no one hears it, does it make a sound? 

Philosophy helps us understand the world, morality, and even ourselves.
