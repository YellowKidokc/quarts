Many-Worlds Interpretation - A quantum mechanics interpretation suggesting that all possible alternative histories and futures are real, each representing an actual "world" or "universe."

 Block Universe (Eternalism)
Core Concept
The Block Universe model proposes that past, present, and future all exist simultaneously in a fixed four-dimensional spacetime. Time does not "flow"-instead, consciousness moves through a pre-existing structure.
 Implication: Free will may be an illusion, and every moment is as "real" as any other.

Key Elements
Timeless Structure: All moments exist at once-much like a movie reel ️ where all frames exist, but we only view one at a time.
Illusion of Flow: The passage of time is just our perception moving through spacetime, much like how a flashlight moving across a mural makes different parts visible.
Spiritual-Physical Connection
If all time exists simultaneously, then God's perspective may be outside time itself.
This aligns with divine omniscience-God sees all moments at once rather than experiencing events in sequence.

 Psalm 90:4 - "A thousand years in your sight are like a day that has just gone by."
