The proposed framework emerges from a critical insight: our understanding of reality is not confined to discrete disciplinary domains, but exists as an interconnected web of meaning-making processes.

Consciousness operates as a dynamic field of gravitational potential, where:

- Spiritual experiences generate measurable psychological "mass"
- Transformative insights create their own gravitational wells of meaning
- Redemptive processes can be understood as deliberate alterations of existential trajectory
