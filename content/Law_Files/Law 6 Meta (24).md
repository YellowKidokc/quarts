**Physical Concepts:**

- **Quantum Probability Collapse:** The act of choice finalizes one out of many potential realities.

**Spiritual Concepts:**

- **Sowing and Reaping:** The biblical and universal principle that what is planted grows and returns.

- **Divine Cause:** The theological basis that **God's law establishes immutable moral structures** in reality.
