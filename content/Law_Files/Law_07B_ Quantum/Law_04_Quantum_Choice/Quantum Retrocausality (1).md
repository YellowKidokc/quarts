[[Quantum/Quantum]] Retrocausality - The controversial idea that quantum effects might work backwards in time, with future events influencing past ones.

 [[Quantum/Quantum]] Retrocausality
Core Concept
Some experiments suggest that future actions may affect past events.
 Implication: Cause and effect may work in reverse at the quantum level.

Key Elements
Time Influence: The past may not be fixed-it could be altered by future events.
Delayed Choice Experiments: Observing a particle now might affect how it behaved in the past.
Spiritual-Physical Connection
If future choices influence the past, could divine intervention change history based on prayer and faith?
Some interpretations of prophecy suggest God knows outcomes but allows intervention to shift them.
