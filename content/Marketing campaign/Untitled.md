# Publishing and Distribution Options for Articles Connecting Physics and Theology

## 1 Scope of the uploaded articles

The uploaded manuscripts (e.g., **"The Theories We Unified: How Twelve Major Scientific Frameworks Reveal a Single Divine Reality"** and the _Theophysics_ files) use mathematical arguments, physics concepts (e.g., information theory, quantum mechanics and general relativity) and Christian theology to argue that physical laws mirror spiritual realities. They target readers who are comfortable with mathematics, philosophy and theology and often frame religious claims in scientific language. Because the pieces mix academic reasoning with devotional writing, they can appeal to multiple audiences-academics interested in science-and-religion dialogues, faith communities, and broader philosophically minded readers.

## 8 Suggestions for guerrilla marketing

1. **Package your content for different audiences.** Create a short press release summarising the key claim (e.g., "New research argues that physical laws mirror spiritual grace"), linking to a longer article hosted on your blog or Medium.
