#### **1. The Discipline of the Pause (Cultivating the Observer)** 

Before you can sort your thoughts, you must first learn to see them without becoming them. The goal here is to What do I do create a moment of separation between a thought or feeling (the stimulus) and your reaction to it. This is the foundation of self-awareness.
