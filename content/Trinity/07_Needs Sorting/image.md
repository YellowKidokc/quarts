**How to Use:**

1. **Right-click** on the image you prefer and choose **"Save Image As..."** or **"Copy Image"**.
    
2. **Paste or insert** the image into your document (Word, Google Docs, Obsidian, etc.).
    
3. **Resize** the image within your document to take up roughly half the page height or width, as desired.
    

Because it's an image, the font size is fixed relative to the image dimensions, ensuring it stays large and clear when you scale the image up.

Let me know if you need any adjustments to the font size, colors, or layout!
