Gravity is **the invisible force that governs motion across the cosmos.** It is the glue that keeps planets in orbit, galaxies in formation, and everything grounded to the Earth.

>  **G** = Gravitational Constant

>  **r** = Distance Between Them

 **Key Insight:** The **force weakens as distance increases** (_inverse square law_).
