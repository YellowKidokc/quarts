- **Key Terms:**
    
    - **d:** Width of the energy barrier.

    - **ħ:** Reduced Planck constant.
- **Feynman-Style Analogy:**
    
    - Imagine a ball rolling towards a steep hill that, classically, it shouldn't be able to cross. In quantum terms, there's a small chance it "appears" on the other side of the hill-a miracle of probability.
